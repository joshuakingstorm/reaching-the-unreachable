Our Mission

Reaching the Unreachable is dedicated to serving the most vulnerable members of our community by providing essential resources, medical care, education, and spiritual support to those in need across Uganda.

Founded and led by Florence Nalulyo, our organization works tirelessly to extend love, care, and practical support to needy families, sick individuals, orphans, and children who have limited access to basic necessities.

About Us

Reaching the Unreachable is a Ugandan-based charitable organization founded and led by Florence Nalulyo. Our mission is to extend love, care, and practical support to the most vulnerable members of our society.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to serving needy families, sick individuals, orphans, church members, and children across various communities in Uganda. We believe that everyone deserves dignity, hope, and an opportunity to thrive.

Through our various programs and initiatives, we provide food, clothing, medical assistance, education support, and spiritual guidance to those who need it most. Our work is made possible by the generous support of donors and volunteers who share our vision of a more compassionate world.

Our Vision

We envision a Uganda where every person, regardless of their circumstances, has access to basic necessities, education, healthcare, and the opportunity to live a life of dignity and purpose.

Building schools to provide totally free education, Creating health centresmwith free medical services and also rehabilitation of the drug addicts and alcohol consumers.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach every person with empathy and understanding
  • Integrity: We maintain transparency and honesty in all our operations
  • Service: We are dedicated to serving those in need with humility
  • Community: We believe in the power of community to create lasting change
  • Faith: Our work is guided by our faith and commitment to loving our neighbors

Our Impact

Since our founding, we have:

  • Provided education support to over 500 children
  • Distributed food and essential supplies to more than 120 families
  • Organized medical camps serving over 800 people
  • Built partnerships with 15 local churches and community organizations
  • Extended our reach to 30+ villages across Uganda
